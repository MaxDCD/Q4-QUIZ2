NOLI MI TANGERE: CHARACTER LIST

Juan Crisostomo Ibarra y Magsalin

  • Also known as Don Crisostomo Ibarra
  • The protagonist in the story
  • Love interest of Maria Clara
  • The son of Don Rafael Ibarra
  • Came back to the Philippines after 7 years of studying in Europe

Maria Clara de los Santos

  • Also known as Maria Clara
  • Love interest of Don Crisostomo Ibarra
  • The daughter of Kapitan Tiago

Padre Damaso

  • A corrupt priest.
  • Is arrogant and abusive, and uses his powers as a priest to his advantage.
  • Previous priest of San Diego, but changed to Maynila.

Kapitan Tiago

  • The father of Maria Clara
  • One of the wealthiest and influential people in San Diego
  • The governor of a town mostly comprised of mestisos

Padre Salvi

  • Looks sickly and pale
  • The new priest of San Diego
  • Unlike Padre Damaso who physically abuses others, he instead makes a fee if you do something wrong.

Pilosopong Tasyo

  • His mother was a wealthy woman
  • His mother thought that his studies was making him forget about God, making him decide if he should quit his studies or become a priest.
  • After the death of his mother and wife led him to depression, he decided to cope by studying and buying books, leading to him becoming poor.
  • Those who were not educated called him "baliw" (crazy).

Sisa

  • She is the mother of Basillio and Crispin.
  • In an abusive relationship with Pedro

Basillio

  • The eldest son of Sisa
  • Works and learns in the Sakristian Mayor
  • His goals are to have Pilosopo Tasyo as his teacher, work at Crisostomo Ibarra's, and get Crispin to learn in Maynila in order to fulfil his dream of becoming a doctor
  • After hearing the suffering of his younger brother, he decided to escape the Sakristian Mayor, leading to him being shot and hurt, and leaving Crispin behind.

Crispin

  • The youngest son of Sisa
  • Works and learns in the Sakristian Mayor
  • Was accused of stealing 2 once (32 pesos), leading him to stay at the church until he pays a fee.
  • Dreamed to become a doctor
  • Killed by the sakristian mayor

Tenyente Gueverra

  • Also known as Miguel Gueverra
  • Lieutenant of the guardia civil
  • A close friend of Don Rafael Ibarra
